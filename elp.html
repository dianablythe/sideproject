Being "Time Smart"

Diana could learn to manage her time in the mornings and around sleep much better!

Sometimez she gets so wrapped up in the everyday madness of life she can no longer see the forrest through the trees.

But what if the merry go round of life meant more than just being born, growing up, living to work for dirty green cloth, maybe having a family and then getting old, hoping to be taking care of and live till 80 and then dying in a not so tragic way if you're lucky, deciding to believe in a higher power just in case right before and your spirit floating into the stratosphere?

What if the point of the merry go round was to realize what it is, radically accept it for what it is and learn how to slow it down just enough that you can jump off and maybe land on your feet.

If not do a stop, drop, and roll like we were taught during Stranger Danger as children? Hmm.

Food for thought I suppose...
